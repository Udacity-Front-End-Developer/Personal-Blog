My Bum! My tongue!

by Ichabod | Jan 22 2020

It almost drives me crazy how humans meddle with our daily personal affairs.

For each time when my human chases me away from the living room when I'm in the middle of my "holy clensing ritual" the thought of packing my things all leave him alone in the house grows stronger

What humans fail to understand, is it's of the utmost importance that a cat has to clean his but,but my human gets easily disgusted each time I spread my wings to do my business.

He doesn't understand the anatomy of the cat's tongue, and I don't trust him to clean me up, especially after he tried to murder me once by attempting to drown me in his bath, his right arm bares the marks of what happened that day.
